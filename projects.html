"José"

I created this work of illustration, when I attended the multimedia technician school, as an implementation of the matter of vectorization. Made with the help of Illustrator Software and Photoshop, the name of the project comes from the name of the protagonist of photography.

"Uovo"

It is an illustration that some time ago I did for myself as an application of knowledge, to design a conceptual digital composition with 3D and photography with Cinema 4D and Photoshop. With the help of psdvault

"Typography Portrait"

My typographic portrait. An impressive combination of typography and illustration. I have carefully selected the colors and the dimensions to place it on the page in an original way. With the help of Photoshop and Illustrator.

"Fashion"

Final project in the first year of the multimedia technician school, with the topic fashion photography. The art that unites two worlds: On the one hand the photography itself, and on the other the fashion that is the highest aesthetic sense. This project allowed me to put together the creativity, technique and passion that allows me to make a product known to the general public. It was not easy at all, I had to provide everything from sponsorships, models, scenaries, lights for post-production etc…

"Intro"

Motion graphics work realized as a proposal for an introduction of an acronym in After Effects, for school purpose. This work helped me with the implementation of knowledge in After Effects; from the creation of shapes and animations to the production of video graphics and sound creation. All with the help of vfxbro

"Ainda há uma pastora"

A documentary film made with the support of the EFP school in the audiovisual language. The theme portrays the abandonment of young people from small country villages and the peasant profession that is always less interesting the young people.

"Heineken"

The first introduction to the realistic rendering of an object, in this case the proposal of the school was a bottle of beer. To personalize my work I opted for adding water drops, gravel and caps. With 4D Cinema Software and photoshop.

"Light bulb"

A personal work realized as an exercise of the skills in modeling, texturing and rendering with Cinema 4D. With the help of cgterminal

"Image Based Lightning"

The scenario was created with Autodesk Maya Software. The basic lighting was created using an Image Based Lighting sphere of light created in Maya and then rendered with the Mental Ray plugin. Thanks to this technique the light source is represented by a sphere that surrounds the scene and allows to have softer lights and softer shadows, for a more realistic result.

"Physical Sun Sky"

For this project I modeled with the Maya software and rendered with the method of Physical Sun Sky a scenario, which was useful for outdoors renderings. I have created an environment similar to the night sky, which opted for a much more directional style for the cartoons.

"Canon"

A personal work realized as an exercise of the skills in modeling, texturing and rendering with Cinema 4D. With the help of envatotuts

"La Vendetta"

Design for the second year Computer Animation exam. It was put at our disposal a list of twenty-four cartoons for choosing one to develop and adapt for the needs of a 3D animation product. The work had a duration of approximately 30 seconds. The Project was carried out implementing the knowledge acquired during the training time.

"Fungo"

Creation work of a character in 2D, initially in paper format, after transformed into a vector with the help of Illustrator. After that, the work continued with the help of Adobe Flash to animate the lip synchronization trough an audio voice-over and respective character animation.
